Our Approach

Relationships that
earn the conversation.

We don't begin with a database. We begin with decades of genuine relationships — and the humility to know that the best candidates deserve to be approached with honesty about the opportunity, not just the title.

Access to exceptional, often-unreachable candidates comes not from exclusivity, but from trust. When we reach out to someone who isn't looking, they respond because we've shown up for them — as colleagues, as honest advisors, as people who understand what matters to them at this point in their career.

Deep Industry Fluency

We understand the brief at a granular level — P&L dynamics, governance structures, agency culture — because we've worked in and alongside these organizations for decades. That context makes us better partners and better advocates for both sides.

Earned Access

The most impactful leaders aren't browsing job boards. Over thirty years, we've built relationships with the people who shape the industry — and when we call, they listen. Not because of who we are, but because of how we've always shown up.

Slates That Challenge Convention

We meet your brief — then we bring candidates who expand it. The most transformative hire is sometimes the one you didn't know to ask for. We bring you the expected and the unexpected, and let you decide what the future looks like.

Human Insight at the Center

Senior leaders today are seeking meaning as much as compensation. We take time to understand where each candidate is in their journey — their aspirations, their values, what would make this the right next chapter — and match from that place.

About M+C Talent

Mission + Cause Talent was built on a simple belief: that communications — done with purpose, by the right leaders — can change the way organizations engage with the world. We started this firm because we'd spent decades in the industry watching the difference a single exceptional hire could make.

We bring thirty-plus years of communications experience to every engagement — not as observers, but as practitioners who have hired for, led, and cared about these roles. We know what the work demands. We know what the best leaders look like. And we know what it means to find a role that genuinely fits.

Our job isn't to fill a seat. It's to find the right person — and to make sure they understand why this opportunity might be the one worth saying yes to.

Brad MacAfee

Founder & CEO

Brad brings more than thirty years of communications leadership to every search — including his tenure as CEO of Porter Novelli, where he led one of the industry's most respected global agencies, and as CEO of KAOH Media, a strategic communications firm focused on community engagement and high-stakes public affairs. A lifelong champion of values-led hiring, Brad has been directly involved in hundreds of senior-level placements across agency and corporate environments. His approach is grounded in a simple belief: that the right match — one built on shared values and authentic fit — changes outcomes for organizations and careers alike. Brad is a board leader across multiple civic and industry institutions and holds deep roots in both the Atlanta and national communications communities.

Steve Mallison-Jones

Partner

Steve is one of the most trusted search professionals in the communications and public relations industry, with twenty years of experience placing senior talent across the United States, Europe, and beyond. Before dedicating his career to executive search, Steve served eight years in the Royal Air Force — an experience that shaped his discipline, his integrity, and his genuine commitment to the people he works with. Clients and candidates alike describe Steve as a confidant first and a recruiter second — someone who takes the long view on relationships and delivers placements that last. Steve is a master at the craft of locating, attracting and securing talent. He loves people and life with a passion.
